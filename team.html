Our Story

FROM THE 99% TO HELPING THE 99%

We didn't build The 99 from a boardroom. We built it from experience—as athletes who learned how to create opportunities in a system that wasn't designed for us.

THE ORIGIN OF THE 99

The top 1% of college athletes capture 95% of all NIL money. Millions of athletes are left without guidance, strategy, or opportunity. We've seen the stats, but more importantly, we've lived the reality.

The 99 was founded to change that equation. We believe any athlete—from any school, any sport, with any following—can build a brand and create real opportunities. Because we did it ourselves.

Now we're teaching every athlete how to do the same through speaking engagements, workshops, our online blueprint, and The 99 Sports App.
